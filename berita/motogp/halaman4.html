Halaman 4 - MOGO GP

Dovizioso: the attendance king

The Italian, along with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage for 16 straight years - the third highest on the behind Valentino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) dan Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32 year-old, who ini the last two years has been - on paper - Marc Marques' (Repson Honda Team) most consistent challenger.
